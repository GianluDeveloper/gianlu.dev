About

I’m Gianlu, a full‑stack web developer based in Italy (remote). I enjoy building small, dependable systems: fast websites, clean APIs, and tooling that removes friction for users and teams.

What I do

  • Web apps with pragmatic architecture and clear UX.
  • Performance & accessibility improvements for existing sites.
  • Consent‑gated analytics and tracking setups that match your policies.
  • Backend work (APIs, queues, automation, Linux tooling).

What I don’t do

  • Dark patterns, misleading UX, or “tracking first” implementations.
  • Unreviewable codebases without docs, ownership, or deploy clarity.

Working style

  • Small milestones with quick feedback loops.
  • Written decisions for anything that affects privacy, security, or performance.
  • Accessible by default (keyboard navigation, semantic structure, readable contrast).

Stack highlights

I’m comfortable across the stack: TypeScript/JavaScript, Node.js, Go, Linux, HTTP, and modern static-site tooling. I keep dependencies small and prefer solutions that are easy to host and audit.
